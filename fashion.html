Urban cinematography study

A visual exploration of Paris through dynamic, mobile setups and genre photography — movement, texture, and rhythm of the city captured in situ.

About the project

The series blends street photography and cinematic movement. I focus on how the city shapes frames: light gradients, narrow passages, and transient action. Mobility setups let me pass through tight streets and changing crowds while preserving clean composition and consistency of motion.
