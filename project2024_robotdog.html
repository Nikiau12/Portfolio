France

Urban cinematography study

A visual exploration of Paris through dynamic, mobile setups and genre photography — movement, texture, and rhythm of the city captured in situ.

About the project

This project brings together photographs I took during my four years living in France. It includes different genres of photography. My goal is to share my photos with you. Through the use of colour, light and other photographic techniques, I convey what I see around me and how I feel about it.
